# Informed Consent for Use of Health Data in Research

**Project/Study Title:** Smart Health Data Exchange (SDX)  
**Institution/Organization:** SDX Open Source Initiative  
**Version Date:** 2025-10-10

---

## 1. Key Information
You are being asked to allow the use of your health information for research.  
Before you decide, please read this form carefully and ask any questions you may have.  
Your participation is completely voluntary.

---

## 2. Purpose of the Research
The purpose of this research is to improve understanding of health conditions and care delivery by analyzing de-identified patient data.  
Your data may help researchers develop better diagnostic tools, improve treatment options, and support innovations in digital health and telemedicine.

---

## 3. Procedures
If you agree to participate:
- Researchers will use health data that has already been collected as part of your normal care.
- No additional tests, visits, or procedures are required.
- Your data will be de-identified before use in research, meaning that personal details like your name or address will be removed.
- Data may be shared with approved research collaborators or institutions under strict confidentiality agreements.

---

## 4. Risks and Discomforts
There are no physical risks to you.  
Although all possible measures will be taken to protect your privacy, there is a minimal risk that shared data could be re-identified.  
All systems follow industry-standard security practices to reduce this risk.

---

## 5. Benefits
There may be no direct benefit to you personally.  
However, your contribution may help researchers and clinicians:
- Enhance medical knowledge,
- Improve health data systems, and
- Develop safer and more effective healthcare solutions.

---

## 6. Confidentiality and Data Protection
- Identifying details will be removed or replaced with coded study IDs.  
- Data will be encrypted and stored securely.  
- Only authorized members of the research team or approved partners will have access.  
- All processing will comply with privacy and data protection laws (e.g., HIPAA, GDPR).  

---

## 7. Voluntary Participation
Your participation is completely voluntary.  
If you choose not to participate, your healthcare services or relationship with your provider will not be affected in any way.

---

## 8. Withdrawal of Consent
You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting the research team.  
After withdrawal, your data will not be used in any new research projects, though it may remain in studies already underway.

---

## 9. Future Use of Data
Please indicate your choice regarding the use of your de-identified data for future research:

- [ ] I give permission for my data to be used for future approved studies.  
- [ ] I do **not** give permission for my data to be used in future studies.
